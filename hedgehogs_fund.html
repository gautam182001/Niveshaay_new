Niveshaay Hedgehogs Fund

Niveshaay Hedgehogs Fund is built on a decade of equity investing expertise. Our strategy prioritizes entrepreneurial leadership over professional management, leveraging on-ground insights to identify and seize new, untapped opportunities. Specializing in both listed and unlisted securities, including pre-IPO fundraising, we aim to unlock significant business and entrepreneurial potential.

Business Focus

Our investment thesis is anchored in a deep understanding of the business model, entire value chain and Identification of Proxies

Entrepreneur’s Vision​

We prioritize entrepreneurial leadership, focusing on execution capabilities and key attributes like ethics, capital allocation, resilience, and the entrepreneur's journey

Scuttlebutt Approach

​Leveraging our network of entrepreneurs and investors, we prioritize firsthand insights and on-the-ground research to identify companies poised for sustainable growth

Better Portfolio Construction

A strategic mix between listed and unlisted securities allowing to capitalize across different segments of the market

Pre - IPO Deals & Unlisted Deals

Early access to high-growth opportunities before they are accessible to the broader public market

Anchor / Preferential Investments

AIF as a vehicle qualify for QIBs and participation in preferential issue

Better Execution & Greater Flexibility

Centralized fund pricing will reduce the price impact ensuring all investors benefit equally

Unconventional & Emerging Industry Trends

Our team excels to identify and capitalize on dynamic opportunities that are undiscovered or overlooked

Key Facts

  • Infrastructure Integration and Scalability: Integrating smart meters with existing, often outdated, infrastructure and ensuring they are scalable and compatible with various technologies.
  • Cost Management and Grid Stability: Balancing the costs of deployment, maintenance, and upgrades while maintaining grid reliability, especially in areas with frequent power fluctuations.
